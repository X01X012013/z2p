About This Chat Board

Join our Discord server: discord.gg/9XuRgQn

Please post your comment on the top except when you need to reply someone, this way, your comment will be seen.

When editing your post, please make a new line and write "Edit: " so other people can know that your post was changed.

Be respectful and try to post your comments in the right place.

"Report Issue" is only for issues, suggestions, and complaints related to this website. Game related issues (game bug, game account issues, etc) should be posted to the official forum.

You do not need an account to chat, but if you are a lord and want to be moderator you will need an account and let me know your ID.

You need to enable JavaScript to use this website (not an issue for most of the time).
